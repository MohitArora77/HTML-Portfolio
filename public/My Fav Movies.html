The Best Movies According to Mohit

My top 3 of all-time

Your Name

Your Name (Japanese: 君の名は。, Hepburn: Kimi no Na wa.), stylized as your name., is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, produced by CoMix Wave Films,and distributed by Toho. It depicts the story of high school students Taki Tachibana and Mitsuha Miyamizu, who suddenly began to swap bodies despite having never met,unleashing chaos on each other's lives.

"Treasure the experience. Dreams fade away after you wake up." - My Favourite Quote

A Silent Voice

A Silent Voice (Japanese: 映画 聲の形, Hepburn: Eiga Koe no Katachi, lit.  'Film Shape of Voice') is a 2016 Japanese animated drama film based on the manga of the same name by Yoshitoki Ōima. The film was produced by Kyoto Animation, directed by Naoko Yamada and written by Reiko Yoshida, featuring character designs by Futoshi Nishiya and music by Kensuke Ushio. Plans for an animated film adaptation were announced back in November 2014, Kyoto Animation was confirmed to produce the film in November 2015. Miyu Irino and Saori Hayami signed on as voice casting in May 2016 and the theatrical release poster and official trailer were released in July 2016.

"The Thing Is, You Have To Accept The Bad Parts Of Yourself With The Good And Just Move Forward." - My Favourite Quote

John Wick

John Wick is an American neo-noir action film series and media franchise created by Derek Kolstad. It follows the titular character, portrayed by Keanu Reeves, who is a legendary hitman. The story begins with him coming out of retirement to seek revenge against those who killed his dog, a gift from his late wife, setting off a series of violent events.
